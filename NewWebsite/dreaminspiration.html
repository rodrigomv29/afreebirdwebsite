DREAM INSPIRATION

Our program strives to provide each child, whether fighting cancer or in remission from cancer, with the inspiration to pursue their own personal and artistic DREAM! Through A Free Bird™, these children engage with the flourishing NYC arts community and view the work of visionaries in their chosen artistic disciplines first- hand – whether on stage at a Broadway Theatre, within the legendary Metropolitan Museum of Art, or on a New York Fashion Week runway!

DREAM provides the tickets for a young artist, his or her parental guardian, and an A Free Bird supervisor to attend one of the many artistic venues in New York City! A photo of the group, along with the child and his or her A Free Bird mentor’s reflections on their special experience, will then be uploaded to the official A Free Bird website and Facebook page. The group can then share their artistic journey with family and friends!
