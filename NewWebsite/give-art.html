DESCRIPTION

A Free Bird Organization is a nonprofit organization that aims to help childhood cancer patients fight cancer through the power of therapeutic arts. Each year, around 250,000 children are affected by
cancer worldwide. Your art donations would enhance our mission of helping children with cancer through different forms or therapeutic arts. Here are some examples of art supplies you might consider
donating to us: piano, violin, guitar, flute, paint brushes, paper, pens, pencils, sheets, paints, books, ballet uniforms, ballet shoes, Jazz costumes, software, photoshop, etc. We would be pleased to showcase a
picture of you and your donation, along with a picture of the child/children you’re helping with your donation on our website, if you’d like.
